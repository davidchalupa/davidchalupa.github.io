David Chalupa

A member of the Operations Research Group at Aalborg University.
Previously a member of the Computational Science Research Group (CSRG) and an affiliated member of the Digital Centre at the University of Hull.
Before that a software engineer at ESET.
PhD in applied informatics, with research on stochastic graph algorithms.
Interested especially in complex networks, evolutionary computation and combinatorial optimisation.
